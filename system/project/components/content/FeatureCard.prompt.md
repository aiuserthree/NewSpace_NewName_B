**FeatureCard** — a three-up highlight card with a dark 48px icon, heading, body and optional orange link; depth comes from the warm glow shadow.

```jsx
<FeatureCard
  icon="chart-pie"
  title="한 화면에서 제출"
  body="세 공간의 이름을 스크롤하며 한 번에 제안할 수 있어요."
  linkText="참여 방법 보기"
/>
```

- `icon` takes an Icon name or a custom node. `surface` `card` (white) or `canvas` (cream). `elevated` toggles the warm shadow.
- Body is warm-stone; heading is ink 600. Keep the orange to the link only.
